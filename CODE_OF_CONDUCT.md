# Code Of Conduct

This project should be a useful place for people who care about careful
empirical claims. Please keep discussions precise, respectful, and public-safe.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good faith while asking for evidence and exact boundaries.
- Discuss claims, templates, files, and reproducibility steps rather than
  making personal judgments.
- Keep feedback actionable and tied to public project material.
- Respect confidentiality around papers, reviews, datasets, and institutions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or sustained disruptive comments.
- Sharing confidential paper text, private review notes, credentials, or raw
  data without rights to redistribute it.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to make acceptance, legal, ethics, or
  deployment-safety judgments outside the project boundary.
- Misrepresenting the resource as an autonomous reviewer or paper decision
  system.

## Reporting

If a conduct issue appears in a public thread, flag it in the same venue with a
brief, specific note or contact a maintainer through the repository owner
profile. Keep sensitive details out of public issues when possible.

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close threads, or restrict
participation to keep the project safe and usable.
